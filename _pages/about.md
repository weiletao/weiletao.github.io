I am currently a senior undergraduate student at the College of Software Engineering, Sichuan University (SCU). 

Starting from September 2026, I will pursue my Master's degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT), where I will join the Machine Learning and Data Mining Research Group led by Prof. Qianli Ma.

My research interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Mining, with a particular focus on time series data modeling and representation learning. I am especially interested in developing intelligent methods for understanding complex temporal dynamics and real-world sequential data.

I am actively exploring research opportunities related to:
- Time Series Analysis and Forecasting
- Representation Learning
- Deep Learning for Sequential Data
- AI for Real-world Applications

Feel free to connect with me for academic discussions, collaborations, or research exchanges.
